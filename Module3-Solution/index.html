Sushi

Sushi is made of small pieces of raw fish that are wrapped in rice and seaweed. The seaweed, called nori, is collected with submerged bamboo nets. While some sushi is mass-produced using robots, the best sushi is made by hand. The sushi rolls are prepared by selecting certain types of fish that meet the highest standards for fat contant, color and taste. (End of Sushi session)
